# SmartShala V1 Scope

SmartShala V1 is a school operations ERP for Indian schools. It focuses on internal school staff workflows and WhatsApp-based parent communication.

## In Scope

- Principal/Admin panel with daily visibility.
- Teacher panel for assigned classes.
- Student CRUD and student detail view.
- Class and teacher assignment management.
- Attendance marking by class and date.
- Attendance reports, low attendance flags, and repeat absentee alerts.
- Fee structures, installments, student fee assignments, partial payments, receipts, pending dues, and defaulter list.
- Rule-based AI analytics for attendance and combined attendance/fee risk.
- Principal daily report and monthly report-ready data.
- WhatsApp notification logging and provider abstraction.

## Out of Scope for V1

- Parent login or parent app.
- Exams, LMS, hostel, transport, timetable, online payment gateway, and multi-school super-admin.
- Custom ML models.
- Full PDF generation pipeline.

Parents receive updates through WhatsApp only.
